## Ingredients

- 2 large leeks, washed and sliced
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 2 garlic cloves, thinly sliced
- 2 x 400g tins butter beans, drained
- 180ml vegetable stock
- 2 tbsp creme fraiche
- Small bunch dill, chopped
- 1 tbsp lemon juice
- Salt and black pepper

## Method

1. Fry the leeks in olive oil over medium-high heat until deeply coloured.
2. Add garlic and cook for 30 seconds.
3. Stir in the beans and stock, then simmer for 5 minutes.
4. Fold in creme fraiche, dill, and lemon juice.
5. Season well and serve with toast.

## Notes

Finish with extra olive oil and plenty of black pepper.
