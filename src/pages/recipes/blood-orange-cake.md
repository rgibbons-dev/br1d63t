<h1>Blood Orange Olive Oil Cake</h1>
<p class="recipe-meta">Winter · One 23cm cake</p>

Olive oil keeps this cake plush for days while blood orange adds a gently bitter perfume.

## Ingredients

- 3 blood oranges
- 220g caster sugar
- 3 eggs
- 160ml extra-virgin olive oil
- 200g plain flour
- 1 1/2 tsp baking powder
- 1/2 tsp fine sea salt
- 120g Greek yogurt

## Method

1. Heat oven to 175°C and line a 23cm round tin.
2. Finely grate zest from 2 oranges and squeeze 160ml juice.
3. Whisk sugar and eggs until thick, then whisk in oil and yogurt.
4. Fold in flour, baking powder, salt, zest, and 80ml juice.
5. Bake 40–45 minutes until a skewer comes out clean.
6. Simmer remaining juice to a syrup and brush over warm cake.

## Notes

Serve with crème fraîche and thinly sliced orange segments.
