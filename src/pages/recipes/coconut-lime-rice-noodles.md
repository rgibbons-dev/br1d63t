## Ingredients

- 200g rice noodles
- 1 tbsp coconut oil
- 1 shallot, finely sliced
- 20g ginger, grated
- 2 garlic cloves, grated
- 400ml coconut milk
- 300ml vegetable stock
- 2 tbsp soy sauce
- Juice of 2 limes
- Basil and coriander, to serve

## Method

1. Soften shallot, ginger, and garlic in coconut oil.
2. Add coconut milk, stock, and soy sauce; simmer for 10 minutes.
3. Cook noodles separately according to packet instructions.
4. Divide noodles between bowls and pour over the broth.
5. Finish with lime juice and herbs.

## Notes

Add shredded greens or tofu if you want to make it more substantial.
