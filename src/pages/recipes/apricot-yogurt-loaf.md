## Ingredients

### Pantry

- 180g plain flour
- 1 1/2 tsp baking powder
- 1/2 tsp fine salt
- 150g caster sugar

### Fridge and fruit

- 2 eggs
- 180g Greek yogurt
- 100ml olive oil
- Zest of 1 lemon
- 6 apricots, stoned and chopped

## Method

1. Heat the oven to 175 C and line a loaf tin.
2. Whisk flour, baking powder, and salt in a bowl.
3. In a second bowl whisk sugar, eggs, yogurt, oil, and lemon zest.
4. Fold dry ingredients into the wet, then stir through the apricots.
5. Bake for 45 to 50 minutes until golden and springy.
