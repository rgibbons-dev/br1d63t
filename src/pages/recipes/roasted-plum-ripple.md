## Ingredients

- 8 ripe plums, halved
- 2 tbsp light brown sugar
- 1 strip orange zest
- 1 tsp vanilla extract
- 150ml water
- Thick yogurt or creme fraiche, to serve

## Method

1. Heat oven to 200 C.
2. Arrange plums in a baking dish and scatter with sugar.
3. Add orange zest, vanilla, and water.
4. Roast 20 to 25 minutes until the fruit has collapsed.
5. Spoon over yogurt while still warm.

## Notes

This is also excellent with porridge the next morning.
