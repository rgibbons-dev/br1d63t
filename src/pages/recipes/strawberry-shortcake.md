## Ingredients

- 450g strawberries, hulled and sliced
- 2 tbsp caster sugar, plus extra to taste
- Finely grated zest of 1 lemon
- 280g self-raising flour
- 1 tbsp baking powder
- 55g cold butter, diced
- 200ml buttermilk
- 300ml double cream
- 1 tsp vanilla extract

## Method

1. Toss the strawberries with sugar and lemon zest, then leave for 20 minutes.
2. Heat the oven to 220 C. Rub flour, baking powder, and butter into coarse crumbs.
3. Stir in buttermilk to form a soft dough. Pat out, cut 6 rounds, and bake for 12 to 14 minutes.
4. Whip cream with vanilla to soft peaks.
5. Split warm biscuits and layer with berries and cream.

## Notes

Best served immediately while the biscuits are still warm and crisp at the edges.
