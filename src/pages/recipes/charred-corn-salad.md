## Ingredients

- 6 ears corn, kernels cut away
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 small red onion, finely chopped
- 1 red chilli, finely sliced
- Juice of 2 limes
- 80g feta, crumbled
- Small bunch coriander, chopped
- Salt and black pepper

## Method

1. Heat a wide pan until very hot and char the corn in batches.
2. Toss warm corn with onion, chilli, and lime juice.
3. Add olive oil, feta, and coriander.
4. Season and leave 10 minutes before serving.

## Notes

Good beside grilled fish, roast chicken, or tucked into lunch boxes.
