## Ingredients

- 8 eggs
- 3 tbsp double cream
- 60g feta or soft goat cheese
- Small bunch dill, chopped
- Small bunch parsley, chopped
- 2 spring onions, finely sliced
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- Salt and black pepper

## Method

1. Heat the oven grill to medium.
2. Whisk eggs with cream, herbs, spring onions, salt, and pepper.
3. Warm olive oil in a small ovenproof pan.
4. Pour in the eggs, scatter over the cheese, and cook gently for 5 minutes.
5. Transfer under the grill for 3 to 5 minutes until softly set.

## Notes

Leave it a little under rather than over if you want a tender centre.
