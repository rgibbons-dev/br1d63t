Roasting the squash first deepens the flavor and keeps the soup silky, not watery.

## Ingredients

- 1.2kg squash, peeled and cubed
- 1 large onion, sliced
- 4 garlic cloves
- 3 tbsp olive oil
- 1 litre vegetable stock
- 40g butter
- 8 sage leaves
- 2 tbsp crème fraîche
- Salt and black pepper

## Method

1. Roast squash, onion, and garlic with olive oil at 220°C for 30 minutes.
2. Transfer to a pot, add stock, and simmer 10 minutes.
3. Blend until very smooth and season to taste.
4. Brown butter with sage leaves in a small pan.
5. Serve soup with a spoon of crème fraîche and sage butter.

## Notes

Keeps 3 days in the fridge and tastes even better the next day.
